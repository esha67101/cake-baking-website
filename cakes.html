Milk Cake Recipes

Classic Tres Leches Cake

A rich and moist cake soaked in a blend of three milks. Perfectly sweet and delicious.

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  • Combine with eggs and milk, bake for 30 minutes.
  • Soak with a mixture of condensed milk, evaporated milk, and cream.
  • Chill and serve with whipped cream.

Coconut Tres Leches Cake

Delightfully creamy and flavorful with a hint of coconut. An exotic twist on the classic recipe.

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • Prepare the batter with coconut milk and shredded coconut.
  • Bake for 30 minutes and let cool.
  • Soak with coconut milk, condensed milk, and evaporated milk.
  • Top with toasted coconut flakes and whipped cream.

Chocolate Tres Leches Cake

A decadent chocolate version of the classic tres leches cake. Rich and indulgent.

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • Mix cocoa powder into the flour mixture.
  • Bake for 30 minutes and cool.
  • Soak with chocolate milk, condensed milk, and evaporated milk.
  • Top with chocolate ganache and whipped cream.

Pancake Recipes

Classic Fluffy Pancake

Delight in the airy, fluffy texture of these classic pancakes, perfect for a leisurely breakfast or a sweet brunch treat.

  • Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  • Mix in milk, eggs, and melted butter until just combined.
  • Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  • Pour batter onto the skillet and cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  • Serve warm with maple syrup and a dusting of powdered sugar.

Banana Pancake

Enjoy the natural sweetness and extra moistness of these banana pancakes, an irresistible twist on the classic breakfast favorite.

  • Mash ripe bananas and mix with flour, sugar, baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  • Stir in milk, an egg, and melted butter until the batter is smooth.
  • Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  • Pour batter onto the skillet, cooking until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  • Top with fresh banana slices and a drizzle of honey or syrup.

Blueberry Pancake

Brighten up your morning with these blueberry pancakes, bursting with juicy berries and a hint of vanilla, perfect for a refreshing start to your day.

  • Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  • Mix in milk, an egg, melted butter, and a splash of vanilla extract.
  • Gently fold in fresh or frozen blueberries.
  • Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  • Pour batter onto the skillet, cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden brown. Serve with a sprinkle of powdered sugar.

Cupcake Recipes

Vanilla Cupcakes

Delight in the airy, fluffy texture of these classic pancakes, perfect for a leisurely breakfast or a sweet brunch t Delight in the simplicity and elegance of vanilla cupcakes, where each bite offers a buttery, vanilla-infused taste that's both classic and comforting.

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  • Cream together 1/2 cup unsalted butter and 1 cup sugar until light and fluffy.
  • Beat in 2 large eggs one at a time, then add 1 tsp vanilla extract.
  • Mix in 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour, 1 1/2 tsp baking powder, and 1/2 cup milk alternately until smooth.
  • Bake for 18-20 minutes and cool before frosting with vanilla buttercream.

Chocolate Cupcake

Dive into a rich, velvety chocolate experience with these decadent chocolate cupcakes that promise a burst of flavor with every bite.

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  • In a bowl, whisk together 1 cup all-purpose flour, 1 cup sugar, 1/2 cup cocoa powder, 1 tsp baking powder, 1/2 tsp baking soda, and 1/4 tsp salt.
  • Add 1/2 cup melted butter, 1/2 cup milk, 2 large eggs, and 1 tsp vanilla extract; mix until smooth.
  • Pour batter into liners, filling 2/3 full, and bake for 20-22 minutes.
  • Cool completely before frosting with chocolate ganache or your favorite icing.

Lemon Cupcake

Brighten your day with zesty lemon cupcakes, featuring a perfect balance of tart and sweet that will leave your taste buds refreshed and craving more.

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  • Cream 1/2 cup unsalted butter with 1 cup sugar until fluffy, then mix in 2 large eggs.
  • Add the zest of 1 lemon and 1/4 cup lemon juice, then stir in 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour and 1 1/2 tsp baking powder.
  • Mix in 1/2 cup milk until combined and smooth.
  • Bake for 18-20 minutes, cool, and frost with lemon glaze or your preferred frosting.
