Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

  • Mix butter, sugar, and eggs until smooth.
  • Stir in flour and chocolate chips.
  • Shape into balls and place on a baking tray.
  • Bake at 180°C for 12-15 minutes.
  • Let them cool and enjoy!

Peanut Butter Cookie Recipe

  • Cream together butter, sugar, and peanut butter.
  • Mix in eggs and flour.
  • Form into balls and flatten with a fork.
  • Bake at 180°C for 10-12 minutes.
  • Cool before serving.

Oatmeal Cookie Recipe

  • Cream together butter and sugar.
  • Mix in eggs, oats, and flour.
  • Add a touch of cinnamon for flavor.
  • Bake at 175°C for 10-12 minutes.
  • Allow to cool on a wire rack.
